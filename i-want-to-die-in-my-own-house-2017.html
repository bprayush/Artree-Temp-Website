I want to die in my own house
 October 4 - November 17, 2017  Srihatta Samdani Art Center and Sculpture Park, Sylhet, Bangladesh

“I want to die in my own house” - Subas Tamang

(Subas Tamang was commissioned to work on a sculpture for the Sculpture Park)

Tamang’s work is a continuation of a project he started during ArTree’s Open Studio. But the commissioned work is on a much larger scale - he used three stone roofs instead of one. One of the roods has a portrait of his father and mother; another one has a portrait of a working-class family from the Sylhet area who dream of building their own house one day; and the center roof contains expressions of people from Nepal and Bangladesh about their thoughts and feelings associated with the word 'ghar' (home). The issues of labour migration and the ensuing mental, physical and emotional states related to having an impermanent existence is a reality faced by millions across the South Asia region, not only in Nepal and Bangladesh.
